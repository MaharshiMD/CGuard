Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Your privacy and the security of your codebase are our absolute highest priorities. This Privacy Policy describes our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use the CodeGuard AI service, API, and associated applications.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name and email address to manage your profile and scan history.

Source Code Data: Code uploaded to our platform for evaluation is temporarily stored in memory during AST compilation and analysis. For typical users, the original source code is purged from our systems immediately after report generation, leaving only anonymized, structural fingerprints.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To construct semantic comparisons and provide our core code forensics service.
  • To notify you about critical security updates, changes to your account, or billing events.
  • To improve our scanning models and infrastructure performance analytically.
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and prevent fraudulent or abusive API use.

3. Code Privacy & Intellectual Property

CodeGuard AI DOES NOT claim ownership of your source code. We strictly prohibit the use of your proprietary, un-anonymized codebase to train our underlying machine learning models without your explicit opt-in consent.

4. Data Security

We implement strict, commercially reasonable security measures to protect your information, including end-to-end TLS encryption for all API traffic, database encryption at rest, and rigorously enforced access controls. While we safeguard your data tightly, no transmission methodology over the Internet is 100% unconditionally secure.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data or codebase to third-party data brokers. We only share information with robustly vetted infrastructure vendors (e.g., cloud hosting providers, transactional email services) that strictly comply with highly secure standards to the exact extent required to run the service effectively.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or data erasure requests concerning this Privacy Policy, please contact our Data Protection Officer immediately at privacy@codeguard.ai.
