Our Story

Securing the world's codebase through advanced AST analysis.

Our Mission

At CodeGuard AI, we believe in the integrity of software development. As the software landscape evolves with the advent of AI coding assistants and global code-sharing, traditional text-matching approaches to plagiarism detection have become obsolete.

Our mission is to provide an airtight, logic-based detection system that protects intellectual property, maintains academic integrity, and empowers organizations to verify the authenticity of their codebase.

Why We Built This

We noticed a critical gap in the market. Existing solutions like Turnitin, MOSS, and JPlag were either outdated, slow, or easily circumvented by basic variable renaming or AI generation. We set out to build a platform that understands code the way compilers do—through Abstract Syntax Trees (ASTs)—and applies deep learning to identify logic similarity regardless of the programming language or structural obfuscation.

Whether a student translates a Python script to Java or a bad actor steals proprietary algorithms and scrambles the variables, CodeGuard AI finds the logical fingerprint to uncover the truth.
