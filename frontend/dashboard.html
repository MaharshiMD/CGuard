Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI detection?

CodeGuard uses advanced fingerprinting and LLM analysis. Our accuracy rate is over 94% on common programming languages.

Is my code stored on your servers?

We prioritize privacy. By default, scans are processed locally or via secure API calls and results are stored in your browser's LocalStorage.

How do I export results to PDF?

Click 'View Report' in your history, then use the 'Download PDF' button at the top of the report page.
